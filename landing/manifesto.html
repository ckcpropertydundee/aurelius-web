It’s the 14th. Can I get my rents that came in on the 1st?

You’ve been waiting two weeks to see money that’s already sitting in your agent’s client account. It’s not a banking delay. It’s their process. They send statements when they feel like it. Batch payments when it suits them, not a service. You’re not a client. You’re a line on a spreadsheet.

Aurelius: Rent visible in real time. Not when someone gets around to it.
Why are you paying 12% for spreadsheets and phone calls?

Management fees of 10–15% made sense when agents had physical offices, paper filing systems, and no internet. Today they use WhatsApp to contact tenants and email to send you a PDF. The infrastructure cost is near zero. The fee is the same. Someone is benefiting from that gap. It isn’t you.

Aurelius replaces the process. Not just the paperwork.
Why are you spending over the agreed contractual sum limit?

Your management agreement almost certainly has a spend limit — usually £100 to £250 — above which authorisation is required. Invoices routinely exceed it without a call. They forgot. They were busy. The contractor quoted more on the day. You find out when the statement arrives. This is not an edge case. This is standard practice.

Every Aurelius invoice is checked against your agreed cap. Automatically.
Who actually spoke to my tenant about the repair?

No one can tell you. There’s no record. A note in someone’s phone, a WhatsApp message on a personal device, a verbal conversation in a corridor. Your property, your legal liability — managed on personal communication tools with zero audit trail. And when something goes wrong, that absence of documentation is your problem, not theirs.

Aurelius: Every communication logged. Every action item trackable.
What we believe

Property management should feel like a financial system — precise, transparent, and automatic. Not like a service industry from the 1980s running on goodwill and paper receipts.
