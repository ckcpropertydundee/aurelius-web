Concept 05 · The Anti-Letting Agency Manifesto

The letting agency model is broken.

We built something better.

01
"They charge 12% and I still have to chase them for updates."

Letting agents take a significant cut of your rental income and still expect landlords to manage the relationship. Phone calls go unreturned. Emails disappear into inboxes. You are paying for a service that requires constant supervision to function at all.

Aurelius charges a transparent fixed fee. Full visibility. No chasing required.
02
"I had no idea my tenant reported a maintenance issue three weeks ago."

Traditional letting agents sit between landlord and tenant, filtering information — and doing it poorly. Issues get lost in email chains. By the time you hear about a problem, it has already become an expensive one. The agent, meanwhile, has moved on.

Every report is logged in real time. You see it the moment it is raised.
03
"The contractor they sent charged twice the going rate — and nobody told me."

When letting agents appoint contractors, they rely on preferred supplier lists with inflated day rates — sometimes with undisclosed referral arrangements. You are billed without context, without approval, and without recourse. The agent carries no financial risk. You carry all of it.

Approved contractors. Transparent pricing. No referral fees. No surprises.
04
"At the end of the tenancy, the deposit dispute cost me more than the damage."

Without thorough documentation at every stage of a tenancy, deposit disputes become unpredictable and expensive. Letting agents rarely maintain the evidence required to support a claim — and when the adjudicator rules, it is the landlord who absorbs the loss.

Inventory tracked from day one. Every condition recorded. No ambiguity at the end.
